pearjam

Vote on and enjoy the best songs your friends come up with!

With PearJam, you and your friends can have a playlist you all vote on. Just join a room, give your friends the URL, collaborate, and enjoy the music!

Everyone gets a copy of music queue and everyone can add to it; but the music only plays on the creator's speakers. Perfect for parties where you have just one computer hooked up to a speaker but everyone gives input on what to hear next!

Note that this is all P2P using a new technology called WebRTC. Our servers don't see what happens in your playlist!
